# Risk Gate

Use this guide when classifying approval mode (`batch` vs `inline`).

## LOW risk

Typical:
- personal workflow notes
- simple build updates
- non-controversial lessons

Approval:
- batch review allowed

## MEDIUM risk

Typical:
- product comparisons
- numeric claims from external sources
- trend commentary with factual assertions

Approval:
- inline review if uncertainty exists

## HIGH risk

Typical:
- legal/regulatory claims
- financial/medical claims
- strong public accusations or sensitive policy statements

Approval:
- inline human review mandatory before publish

## Escalation rule

If unsure between two levels, choose the higher risk level.
